RETROGESTURE CAMERA SYSTEM

1. Dual "L" Frame Gesture

Form an "L" shape with the index finger and thumb of both hands. A glowing, dynamic frame will form between them, with the rest of the feed dropping to classic monochrome.

2. Move & Rotate

Move your hands closer or further to resize the frame. Tilt your hands to rotate the frame to match your perspective.

3. Index Fold to Snap

While holding the frame, quickly fold your index finger on either hand (like pulling a trigger) and release to snap a photo inside the frame. It will instantly align and save to the film roll.

Requires webcam access. Hand detection is computed locally on-device.
